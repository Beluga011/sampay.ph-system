Our Drying Algorithm

We evaluate multiple weather factors to determine the perfect drying conditions

1

Temperature Analysis

We check if temperatures are between 18-32°C (65-90°F) for optimal drying. Higher temperatures accelerate evaporation while maintaining fabric integrity.

> 18°C Ideal Minimum
2

Humidity Assessment

Low humidity levels (below 70%) are crucial for efficient drying. We monitor humidity patterns throughout the day.

< 70% Optimal Humidity
3

Precipitation Check

We ensure there's no rain in the forecast for at least 6 hours to keep your clothes dry and prevent rewetting.

0% Rain Chance
4

Wind Factor

Moderate breeze (5-20 km/h) helps accelerate drying by moving moist air away from your clothes.

5-20 km/h Ideal Wind
